About me

My name is Nate Walinder. I am a James Scholar student studying advertising in the Charles H. Sandage Department of Advertising in the College of Media at the University of Illinois. After graduation I am interested in working in programatic advertising or account management.

As an experienced graphic designer, i am always looking for projects to work on or ideas to bring to life. Connect with me for tattoo designs, logos, or photo editing.

In my free time, I also enjoy running, working on my downhill mountain bike, and backpacking our National Parks. I am also an aficionado of espresso and reading books on narrative nonfiction and comparative mythology.
